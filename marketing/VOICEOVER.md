## 90-second LinkedIn narration

Every business owner knows the routine. The CRM has one piece of the story. Accounting has another. Projects, scheduling, documents, and team communication all live somewhere else. The owner becomes the integration layer.

3Stone One starts from a different premise: the owner should open one screen and immediately understand what needs attention, what is making or costing money, who is behind, and what should happen next.

The Executive Overview compares every business in one place. From there, one login opens the operating context for each company. This event venue tracks deposits, upcoming events, and client follow-up. Switch to a salon and the same connected platform now focuses on appointments, stylist utilization, rebooking, average ticket, and retail product sales. A clothing brand sees orders, fulfillment, returns, margins, and low-stock signals.

The AI changes too. It answers from the selected business’s demo records—who has not clocked in, which customers need follow-up, which deposit is overdue, or what the owner should prioritize first.

Customers, work, finance, people, documents, meetings, analytics, and automation stay connected instead of becoming separate islands.

This is a public interactive demo, not a claim that every production integration is already deployed. It is a tangible view of the product experience 3Stone AI is building.

Most software manages one department. 3Stone One is built around the owner. One Login. One Platform. One AI.
